Social Tools to Accelerate Scientific Productivity

we're brewing something good...

Are you frustrated with your experiments not working?

Remember the time that you had no idea why your experiment wasn't working, and you complained to your lab mate about it? BOOM - she told you how to solve your problem! Yet we don't socialize our data often enough. OpenLab is the first tool to make this process seamless, across all your devices. In fact, collaboration has proven to increase productivity, citations, and overall contribution in the sciences.

Productivity Boosts for Everyone!

Lab Members

Get help when you need it the most. Simply post your data as you collect it and watch as your peers and PI contribute to help you in a fix! At the same time, instead of playing Candy Crush on the T, scroll through your lab's data and contribute any time, any place!

Lab Head / PI

We understand how busy you are. OpenLab enables you to quickly and easily view your lab's progress. Easily give input to help your lab members overcome critical problems.

Collaborations

OpenLab enables you to invite collaborators from outside your lab to only your collaborative project. They only view the relevant data stream. Thereby enabling more fluid and transparent communication in your collaborations.
